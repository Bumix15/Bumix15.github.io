Obudu Mountain Resort

The Obudu Mountain Resort sits majestically above the sea level on the Oshie Ridge of the famous Sankwala Mountains. The idyllic tranquility, comfortable climate, beautiful scenery, and breathtaking views have made this place one of the famous tourists’ attractions in Nigeria.

Getting around

Train and bus

You can get to most of the major towns by train from Udu railway Station. There are many buses that do tours to popular locations and tourist attractions.

Car

The easiest way to get around outside of the cities is by car.

Weather

The weather in Nigeria is very unpredictable! It's best to be prepared for any kind of weather, even if it's a nice day!
